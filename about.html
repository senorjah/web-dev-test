About

Nathaniel Gladstone-Lyon is a musical artist, producer, videoeditor, and etc. based out of the LA area, although some days he’s both of these things and some days neither. I can’t decide sometimes, let’s switch to first person. I’m struggling with indecision so I might not even want to be a musician some days. I like to make noises with my mouth and then transfer them into songs, however long this process takes will refine the end result. I want to do something, most of the times, anything and it helps if you give me something to do, because I can get it done. anything and so started to work on albums and songs with no release plan or strategy, with the end goal of getting something done. Born in 2002 and coming into 2023 with a neverending mental tracklist and 100s of unfinished project files you can be rest assured that he’s not Than-X everyday but when the time comes, he will be.
